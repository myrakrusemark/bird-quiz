Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Bird Quiz ("we", "us", or "our") operates the birdquiz.myrakrusemark.com website. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our Service.

Information Collection and Use

Bird Quiz is designed to work primarily client-side. We do not collect personal information directly. However, we use third-party services that may collect information:

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies for:

  • Essential cookies: To remember your quiz preferences and progress (stored locally in your browser)
  • Advertising cookies: Our advertising partners, including Google AdSense, may use cookies to serve personalized ads based on your browsing history

Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

For more information about how Google uses your data, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage to save:

  • Quiz progress and statistics
  • Your quiz settings and preferences
  • Cookie consent preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Content

Our quiz features bird photos and audio recordings sourced from:

  • iNaturalist: Bird photographs (Creative Commons licensed)
  • Xeno-canto: Bird audio recordings (Creative Commons licensed)

When you access this content, your browser makes requests to our content delivery servers. See our Attribution page for full credits.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The quiz functions entirely client-side without requiring account creation or personal data submission.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your local quiz data at any time through your browser settings
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through Google's settings
  • Use browser extensions to block cookies if desired

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
